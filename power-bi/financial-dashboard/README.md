📊 Projet — Financial Performance Dashboard


Dashboard financier basé sur le dataset Microsoft Financial Sample : KPI de revenu total (127,93M), profit total (16,89M) et marge nette (13,21%). Analyse de l'évolution mensuelle revenu/profit révélant un pic saisonnier en octobre, ventilation de la rentabilité par segment client (Government étant le plus profitable) et par pays (France en tête).



Financial dashboard built on the Microsoft Financial Sample dataset: KPIs for total revenue (127.93M), total profit (16.89M), and net margin (13.21%). Monthly revenue/profit trend analysis revealing a seasonal peak in October, profitability breakdown by customer segment (Government being most profitable) and by country (France leading).
